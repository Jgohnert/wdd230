Reserve one of our scooters or jeeps

We thank you for considering reserving one of vehicles. Before you begin your form, A $50 security deposit is required to confirm reservations. After your reservation is complete, a valid drivers license is required in hand at the time of rental. No one under the age of 12 years of age are allowed to ride on the scooters. Be aware that fuel charge is not included with your rental. Unlimited mileage, and liability insurance is included. Damage insurance is available for purchase. Free delivery to Cozumel hotels or Airbnbs for 2+ day rentals is available to all our renters. Please pay via Google Pay, Apple Pay, cash, credit card, debit card, or venmo. Rentals may be returned to either physical location.
