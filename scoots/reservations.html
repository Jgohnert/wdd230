Rental Services and Agreements

We thank you for considering reserving one of our vehicles. Before you begin your form, we would like to inform you that a $50 security deposit is required to confirm reservations. After your reservation is complete, a valid drivers license is required in hand at the time of rental. No one under the age of 12 years of age are allowed to ride on the scooters. There is no fuel charge with your rental. Unlimited mileage, and liability insurance is included. Damage insurance is available for purchase, along with free delivery to Cozumel hotels or Airbnbs for 2+ day rentals. Please pay via Google Pay, Apple Pay, cash, credit card, debit card, or venmo. Rentals may be returned to either physical location.
